Starving for Success in San Francisco: Which Features of a Restaurant Experience Best Predict Its Star Rating?

If you want to open a restaurant in San Francisco and maximize your star rating, how should you choose where to open given 
what you do best? We trained a Random Forest Model on 752 SF restaurants to identify what drives high ratings. Food quality 
and freshness matter most, but their impact varies by neighborhood, meaning success depends not just on what you do well, 
but on opening where diners value it most.
